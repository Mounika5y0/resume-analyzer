What a top-scoring resume looks like

Lead with verbs

Start every bullet with a strong action verb — led, built, shipped, reduced — never \"responsible for\".

Quantify everything

Numbers beat adjectives. \"Increased conversion 34%\" > \"significantly improved conversion\".

One page. Two max.

Recruiters spend 7 seconds per resume. Trim ruthlessly. Every line must earn its place.

ATS-friendly formatting

No tables, no images, no fancy columns. Plain text, standard sections, clean fonts.
